Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

TL;DR: This game stores your progress locally on your device. We don't collect personal information. If you enable ads, ad networks may collect data. You can delete your data anytime.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Space Clicker ("the Game"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally

The Game stores the following data in your browser's local storage:

  • Game Progress: Particles, harvesters, upgrades, tier level, prestige count
  • Statistics: Total clicks, objects destroyed, playtime
  • Settings: Tutorial progress, help preferences
  • Timestamps: Last played time (for offline progress calculation)

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is stored in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to any server.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • Any data that could identify you

2.3 Third-Party Advertising (When Enabled)

When real advertisements are integrated, third-party ad networks may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (for geographic targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, views)
  • Cookies for ad personalization

Ad Networks We May Use:

  • Google AdSense: Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads: Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Game Progress Data

Locally stored data is used exclusively to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Calculate offline earnings when you return
  • Track achievements and statistics
  • Improve your gameplay experience

3.2 Advertising Data (Third-Party)

When ads are enabled, third-party networks use collected data to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud
  • Personalize ad content (with your consent)

4.1 Security Measures

  • All game data stored locally (not transmitted)
  • No server-side storage or databases
  • No user accounts or passwords
  • HTTPS recommended when deployed

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Access & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: Open browser DevTools (F12) → Application → Local Storage
  • Delete Data: Clear browser data or use in-game reset
  • Export Data: Copy from localStorage if needed
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Use ad blocker (though we appreciate your support!)

5.2 Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy

5.3 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings. When DNT is enabled, we will not load tracking scripts (if implemented in the future).

6. Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information at all, children can play safely.

7. International Users

This game can be played worldwide. However, advertising partners may have geographic restrictions. Data storage occurs entirely on your local device, regardless of location.

7.1 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (view localStorage)
  • Delete your data (clear browser storage)
  • Data portability (export from localStorage)
  • Object to processing (disable ads with ad blocker)

7.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

We do not sell your personal information. We don't collect personal information to sell.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of changes.

9. Third-Party Links

The game may display advertisements linking to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

10. Data Retention

Game progress data is retained indefinitely in your browser until you:

  • Clear your browser's data
  • Uninstall your browser
  • Use the in-game reset function (future feature)
